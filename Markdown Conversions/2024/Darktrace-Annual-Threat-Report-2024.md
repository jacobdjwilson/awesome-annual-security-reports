based approach to security.

03 **Vulnerability Management**: Prioritize patching critical vulnerabilities, especially those in internet-facing devices.

04 **Identity Security**: Implement robust Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and monitor for anomalous identity-related behavior.

05 **Network Segmentation**: Segment networks to limit lateral movement, particularly between IT and OT environments.

06 **Employee Training**: Conduct regular security awareness training to help employees identify phishing and social engineering attempts.

07 **Incident Response**: Develop and regularly test incident response plans to ensure a swift and effective response to security incidents.

08 **Collaboration**: Engage with the wider cybersecurity community and share threat intelligence to improve collective defense.

09 **Autonomous Response**: Consider leveraging AI-driven autonomous response technologies to mitigate threats in real-time.

10 **Continuous Monitoring**: Maintain continuous visibility across the entire network to detect and respond to anomalies promptly.

## Community interest efforts

### Paris Olympic Games 2024 and the US presidential election

In 2024, the Darktrace Threat Research team monitored global events for potential cyber threats. The Paris Olympic Games and the US presidential election were two major events that attracted significant attention from threat actors.

- **Paris Olympic Games 2024**: Darktrace observed an increase in phishing campaigns and attempts to compromise infrastructure related to the Games. Threat actors sought to exploit the high-profile nature of the event to conduct espionage and disruption.
- **US Presidential Election**: The election cycle saw a rise in disinformation campaigns, attempts to compromise political organizations, and efforts to influence public opinion. Darktrace’s AI-driven detection helped identify and mitigate several threats targeting election-related infrastructure.

These events highlight the importance of proactive threat hunting and monitoring during periods of heightened geopolitical activity.
