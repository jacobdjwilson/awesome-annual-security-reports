## Objective
The goal of the Snyk Customer Value Study is to understand how much quantifiable benefit customers have realized by leveraging the Snyk platform and how they think about their return on investment.

The goals of this research are to:
- Identify the most important value drivers within customer organizations.
- Explore how well Snyk delivers on these value drivers.
- Understand the quantifiable benefits and return on investment Snyk customers have realized in the past year.

## Methodology
Nearly 550 Snyk customer respondents from small and midsize to Fortune 500 organizations participated in this year's survey. Snyk partnered with Cascade Insights (a B2B market research firm) to design and administer this online survey for existing Snyk customers in 2023.

## Programming language preferences, Snyk tools, and Competitor usage
JavaScript, Python, and Java were the three most popular languages both in 2023 and in 2022.

**The most used Snyk tools are:**
- Snyk Code
- Snyk Open Source
- Snyk Container

**Key Statistics:**
- 74.5% are using 2+ Snyk products.
- 89.4% of respondents report having used alternative solutions prior to bringing on Snyk.
- 80.9% of respondents report having evaluated alternative solutions in the past year and ultimately chose Snyk.

## Executive Summary
Customers report that Snyk has helped drive substantial time savings and risk avoidance in the past year — a 2x increase in ROI from 2022.

- **Customer ROI**: +30 Development FTE (The average equivalent savings gained per organization from risk avoidance and developer productivity gains with Snyk as reported by over 500 customers).

## About Snyk
Snyk understands how developers work & how they want security to work (focusing on finding & prioritizing important issues while making it simple to fix). Snyk allows security teams to focus on governance, automation, and policy instead of managing tickets and creating roadblocks.

The Snyk platform supports scale, working across the entire application stack from code to cloud & delivers the best security results powered by a world class security team, AI/ML, and our developer community.

## Top considerations for leadership
**For CISOs:**
- Risk reduction: 43.8%
- Compliance: 30.9%
- Automation: 28.5%
- Accuracy: 24.2%
- Security depth: 23%
- Reporting: 19.5%

**For CTOs:**
- Risk reduction, automation: 30.4%
- Developer productivity: 30.2%
- Reliability & Performance: 29.7%
- Compliance: 28.2%
- Accuracy: 25.7%
- Ease of Use: 22.2%

## Coverage, Accuracy, and Compliance
- **Coverage**: Snyk covers 19 programming languages including 11 of the 15 top programming languages and IaC formats used most extensively by professional developers.
- **Accuracy**: As of 2023, Snyk’s OWASP score is a 0.72 (on a scale of 0-1), improving 2.2x in less than a year.
- **Compliance**: Snyk’s infrastructure is certified as compliant with PCI, ISO 27001, ISO 27017, and SOC 2 Type II standards and achieved the FedRAMP “In Process” distinction in 2023.

## Automation
The Snyk platform supports DevSecOps principles by integrating directly into developer workflows to automate secure code development. In the past year, customers increased automated remediation by 70% using the Snyk platform.

## Reporting
Enterprise Analytics provides executives as well as AppSec leaders and practitioners a view into the performance of their AppSec program (including their coverage, exposure, management, and prevention).

## Security depth
Snyk’s Vulnerability Database covers 3x more vulnerabilities than the next largest public database and Snyk users detect and remediate issues 47 days faster (on average) than with the next largest vulnerability database. Snyk DeepCode AI utilizes multiple security-specific AI models along with human curation to model 25M+ data flow cases.

## Reliability, performance, and Ease of use
- **Speed**: Customers reported that, compared to alternative solutions, Snyk scans were 2.4x faster on average.
- **Time to Value (TTV)**: Customers reported that on average, it took 12 days from purchase to initial scan. Full implementation took 48 days on average.

## Measuring Success & Snyk Strengths
82.7% of customers report developer process improvements with Snyk vs. prior to Snyk.

## Customer Value with Snyk: Risk Reduction
87.9% of customers report that Snyk has helped reduce risk in their organizations.

## ROI: Risk avoidance
- **Average savings**: $3.49M per customer in the past year based on risk avoidance.
- **Vulnerabilities fixed**: 50M total vulnerabilities fixed by all customers in the past year.

## Shift left progress to maturity with Snyk
Customers report being 1.8x more likely to reach shift left maturity with Snyk as compared to alternatives.

## Customer Value with Snyk: Developer Productivity
82.7% of customers report that Snyk has helped improve developer processes in their organizations.

## ROI: Developer productivity
- **Average savings**: $1.59M per customer in the past year based on automation and developer efficiency gains.
- **Time savings**: 16.4 hours saved per vulnerability found & fixed with Snyk vs. prior to Snyk.

## Benefits of the Snyk Platform & Add-On Services
75.6% of platform customers report that they’ve been able to eliminate at least one alternative tool by consolidating with Snyk.

## Experience with Alternatives
On average, customers report that they’re able to achieve 56% higher developer adoption with Snyk as compared to alternatives. 78.9% of respondents are “very satisfied” or “satisfied” with Snyk.
